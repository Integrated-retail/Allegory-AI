About Allegory AI

Allegory AI leverages artificial intelligence to provide actionable insights and enhance customer experiences in retail. Key capabilities include:

  • Conversational Clienteling: AI interacts naturally with shoppers, answering questions, offering suggestions, and delivering real-time support.
  • Unmatched Convenience: Customers can quickly scan a QR code to access Allegory AI without downloads or waiting.
  • Real-time Learning: AI adapts instantly to your store’s catalog and inventory, providing accurate availability and cross-selling ideas.
  • Actionable Insights: Capture rich customer data and preferences to inform merchandising and boost engagement.

How Allegory AI Works

  • Scan QR Code: Customers launch Allegory AI by scanning a QR code in-store—no app download required.
  • Enter Your Query: Customers input product questions; AI responds instantly with detailed answers.
  • Point at the Product: AI provides real-time reviews, promotions, and recommendations as customers browse.
